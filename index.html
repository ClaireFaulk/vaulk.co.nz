Concept for Feedback

We are starting with a front-access letter and parcel box - ideal for driveways, fences, and urban sites where rear access isn't an option. Note that additional solutions for large parcel capture will be considered at a later stage.

Moana is our debut concept: a sleek parcel box, purpose-built for New Zealand's coastal environments and ready to mount on fences walls, or posts. Designed for home owners requiring a slim design capable of keeping letters and small parcels safe. Crafted from marine-grade stainless steel for superior corrosion resistance, it's finished in Dulux Duralloy powercoat colours - backed by a 10-year warranty for both durability and colour integrity. And when it's time for a refresh or repair, Moana can be recoated in its original shade or a new look.

Moana's dimensions

  • Pull the "Mail & Parcels" tab to open the anti-theft hatch and deposit mail
  • Mail drops down the chute and lands on a soft pad in the lockable collection area at the bottom
  • Fits A4 letters and parcels up to 280mm x 290mm including NZ Post's Single Bag range XS and S
  • Welded corner seams to prevent water ingress
  • Easy installation - 2 screws
  • Made from 316 marine-grade stainless steel
  • Can colour match with exterior elements such as the roof, doors, and fences for a cohesive look
  • Slim to avoid obstruction of pathways or driveways
  • Minimal design to suit a wide range of archtectural styles

About Us

I live in Lower Hutt and have developed a bit of an obsession with letter and parcel boxes. They sit at the front of our homes, yet so often they're ugly, flimsy, and impractical. Mine's a cheap metal box - the lid lifts in the wind, rain gets in, and I'm regularly greeted by soggy mail, rust and faded paint. It's barely lasted, and that doesn't sit well with my values around sustainability and quality.

Parcels are another headache. Even small ones don't fit, so couriers wrestle with my gate to leave them by the door - sometimes leaving it swinging open. I worry about my parcels when I'm not home.

Surely, I thought, someone could design something better. So I teamed up with a local metal designer and began developing Moana - our first concept. In August 2025, I started testing the market's appetite for secure, architecturally designed letter and parcel solutions. Your feedback means a lot at this early stage. Thanks for being part of the journey.

Claire Faulk
